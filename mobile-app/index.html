About Us

WHealth Calculator Platform

WHealth Calculator

Integrated Health & Wealth Intelligence Platform

🎯 Our Mission

WHealth Calculator is engineered to provide precise, private, and offline-first calculators for body composition, localized diet planning, food tracking, and financial portfolio management.

🍎 Health Planners Suite

  • Health Metrices & Diet Planner: Calculates BMR, TDEE, macronutrient splits, and localized full-day meal charts.
  • Track Food & Nutrition Insights: Log daily meals across All Meals, analyze micro & macro breakdowns in Insights, and manage custom Saved Meals.

📈 Wealth & Finance Suite

  • SIP & Lumpsum: Compound investment growth projections with annual step-ups.
  • SWP Planner: Systematic withdrawal longevity & portfolio sustainability.
  • Loan EMI & Retirement: Amortization schedules and inflation-adjusted corpus calculators.
  • Portfolio Risk Allocation: Age & tenure glide-path risk index scoring.

🔒 100% Private & On-Device

All data calculations and stored meals run entirely inside your device's local sandbox (`localStorage`). Zero tracking, zero telemetry, no user sign-in required.

Legal & Safety

🔒 Privacy Promise: Your Data Stays on Your Device Unlike other trackers, WHealth is engineered to run entirely inside your device's sandbox. We do not transfer your personal, fitness, or financial data to any external server or database.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

When you input parameters, select locations, or calculate financial goals, we do not collect or log your inputs, and we do not require account registration.

2. Local Storage

We use local storage only to remember your unit preferences (metric/imperial) and theme selection (dark mode) for seamless UX.

3. No Trackers

We use zero marketing cookies, pixel trackers, or advertising scripts.
